10 Must-Try Family-Favorite Dinner Recipes

Published: 26th April 2024

When it comes to dinnertime, Indonesian families love meals that are flavorful, comforting, and bring everyone together over delicious shared dishes. These classic recipes have become beloved staples in many households, delivering familiar flavors and Indonesian flair that both kids and adults crave. From aromatic curries and noodle dishes to grilled satays and fritters, these are 10 family-favorite Indonesian dinner recipes worth adding to your regular rotation.

1. Authentic Nasi Goreng (Indonesian Fried Rice)
You can't go wrong with this national favorite dish. Fried rice is stir-fried with meats, vegetables, sweet soy sauce, and savory shrimp paste for an umami blast of flavor in every bite. It's comforting yet incredibly tasty.

2. Indonesian Corn Fritters (Bakwan Jagung)
These addictively crispy corn fritters make for the perfect potluck, appetizer or side dish. Battered corn kernels are fried to golden perfection and served with a zesty peanut dipping sauce. 

3. Opor Ayam (Chicken Cooked in Coconut Milk)
Chicken simmers in an aromatic coconut milk-based curry for a dish that's both comforting and bursting with flavor. This classic central Javan curry gets its bright yellow hue from turmeric and other warming spices. 

4. Sate Lilit (Balinese Minced Seafood Satay)
Grilled seafood satays are a refreshing twist on the classic Indonesian street food. These sate lilit wrap minced fish around lemongrass skewers, creating delicious, chargrilled seafood on a stick.

5. Beef Rendang
No list of Indonesian favorites would be complete without this iconic dish. Beef is slowly simmered in a rich, spiced coconut milk curry until fall-apart tender and caramelized to perfection.

6. Mie Goreng (Indonesian Fried Noodles)
These stir-fried curly noodles are an addictive anytime dish. Yellow egg noodles are wok-tossed with chicken, vegetables, eggs, and a sweet-savory sauce for maximum flavor.

7. Gado-Gado (Indonesian Vegetable Salad with Peanut Sauce)
This bright, veggie-packed salad is drizzled with a creamy, slightly sweet peanut sauce dressing. It makes for a refreshing, high protein vegetarian dish.

8. Soto Ayam (Indonesian Chicken Noodle Soup)
When cravings for a comforting chicken noodle soup strike, this hugely popular Indonesian version really hits the spot. Turmeric-spiced chicken and noodles swim in an aromatic, herby broth.

9. Lumpia (Indonesian Egg Rolls)
These crispy deep-fried spring rolls are the perfect hand-held snack or starter. Savory fillings like cabbage, carrots, and chicken are wrapped in a crispy shell.

10. Tahu Telur (Tofu and Egg Omelet)
This humble yet tasty dish combines protein-packed eggs with cubes of fresh tofu that are lightly crispy on the outside but custard-soft inside. It's pure savory comfort food.

With flavors ranging from aromatic curries to addictive street food bites, these Indonesian specialties offer a crowd-pleasing array of family dinner ideas. Packed with spices, fresh ingredients, and lots of iconic flair, these dishes are sure to become new household favorites.
